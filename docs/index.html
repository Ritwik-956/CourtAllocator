Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

CourtAllocator ("we", "our", or "us") is dedicated to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we treat your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Offline/Local Data: CourtAllocator is designed to function primarily as an offline tool. All data you input (such as player names, scores, and court allocations) is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit this data to any external servers.

Personal Information: We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information such as your email address, phone number, or physical address to use the app.

2. Third-Party Services

The app is built using Expo and React Native. While the app itself does not collect data, it may be subject to the standard data collection practices of the Google Play Store or Apple App Store (e.g., crash reports, install analytics) as part of their platform services.

3. Data Security

Because your data is stored locally on your device, the security of that data depends on your device's security settings. We recommend using standard device security (passcodes, biometric locks) to protect your information.

4. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since all data is stored locally and we do not have access to it, we do not monitor the age of our users.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
